[b]Better Crafting[/b] completely overhauls Wayward's crafting system. It replaces the default prompt with a precision dialog designed to save you clicks, protect your valuable items, and make massive crafting batches safer and easier to control.

[h3]How to Use[/h3]
Getting started is completely seamless. Just open your normal Wayward crafting menu and [b]click on any item you want to craft[/b]. The Better Crafting UI will automatically pop up.

[i](The hotkey behavior depends on your settings: it can either [b]bypass[/b] Better Crafting or [b]access[/b] Better Crafting. By default, holding [b]Shift[/b] bypasses the mod and uses vanilla crafting.)[/i]

[h3]Quality of Life & Time Savers[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Exact Material Selection:[/b] Normal crafting lets you choose exactly which components get used instead of accepting whatever vanilla picks for you.
[*][b]Smart Auto-Fill:[/b] When the window opens, the mod automatically pre-selects the best available ingredients for you, prioritizing your highest quality items first. You are always free to change the selected materials, or you can just hit "Craft" to finish in a single click.
[*][b]Smart Memory:[/b] If a craft fails or only consumes part of your materials, the window remembers exactly what you had selected so you can instantly try again without re-clicking.
[*][b]Exclusion-Based Bulk Crafting:[/b] Instead of clicking 50 items to include them in a batch, simply click the few materials you want to [b]exclude[/b]. The mod will automatically cycle through everything else to craft as many as possible.
[*][b]Instant Results Panel:[/b] See exactly what you made, whether it failed, and the stats/quality of the finished items right inside the crafting UI. No need to dig through your inventory or the chat log.
[/list]

[h3]Major Features[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Normal Crafting Control:[/b] Select your components exactly for single crafts, including tools, consumed materials, and base items where applicable.
[*][b]Bulk Crafting:[/b] Queue up larger crafting runs with quantity controls, per-slot exclusions, and tool selection.
[*][b]Dismantling Support:[/b] Use the same Better Crafting workflow when dismantling items, including target selection and required-item handling.
[/list]

[h3]Survival & Safety Guards[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Health Protection:[/b] Bulk crafting can stop as soon as your health drops, helping prevent dangerous unattended loops.
[*][b]Emergency Interrupts:[/b] Bulk crafting happens turn-by-turn so enemies can still approach. To keep you alive, your bulk crafting batch will instantly abort if:
[list]
[*]You take any damage or lose HP from any source.
[*]You move your character.
[*]You close the menu (Esc) or click Stop.
[/list]
[*][b]Protected Item Handling:[/b] Items marked as "Protected" in your inventory are automatically locked out of bulk batches to keep them safe, but you can still use them manually in the single crafting interface.
[*][b]Durability Protections:[/b] Bulk crafting and dismantling can preserve tools or required items so they do not get worn down when you want to save them.
[/list]

[h3]Deep Information at a Glance[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Hotkey Inspect:[/b] Hold your configured hotkey while hovering over an ingredient to see its exact durability, weight, groupings, and action tier levels.
[*][b]Pre-Craft Info Card:[/b] See the difficulty, required skill, and base stats of the item you are [i]about[/i] to make before you actually commit the resources.
[*][b]Availability Counts:[/b] Recipe sections show how many valid items are currently available, making it easier to plan around your real inventory.
[/list]

[h3]Configurable Settings[/h3]
Customize your experience via the Wayward mod settings menu:
[list]
[*][b]Activation Mode:[/b] Choose whether holding the configured hotkey [i]bypasses[/i] Better Crafting (default) or [i]accesses[/i] Better Crafting.
[*][b]Custom Hotkey:[/b] Set the activation key to [b]Shift[/b], [b]Control[/b], or [b]Alt[/b].
[*][b]Unsafe Bulk Crafting:[/b] Enable this to skip stamina limits and damage-based safety stops during bulk crafting.
[/list]

[h3]Important Notes[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Multiplayer is not currently supported.[/b]
[/list]
